This Cookie Policy explains how SchoolBlocks ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our websites, use our mobile applications, or interact with our educational platform services.

Quick Summary: We use cookies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze how our platform is used. You can control cookie settings through your browser or our cookie consent banner.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language, login status, and other settings.

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may also use:

  • Web Beacons: Small transparent images that help us understand user behavior
  • Local Storage: Browser storage that persists longer than session cookies
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that expires when you close your browser
  • Pixels: Tiny images used for tracking and analytics

Why We Use These Technologies

These technologies help us:

  • Provide a better, more personalized experience
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how our platform is being used
  • Improve our services and fix technical issues
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies Always Active

These cookies are necessary for our platform to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. Without these cookies, our services cannot work correctly.

Examples: Authentication tokens, session identifiers, security cookies

Analytics Cookies Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our services and user experience.

Examples: Google Analytics, usage statistics, performance monitoring

Functional Cookies Optional

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They remember your preferences, such as language settings, theme choices, and customized layouts.

Examples: Language preferences, theme settings, accessibility options

Marketing Cookies Optional

These cookies track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. We use these sparingly and only with your consent.

Examples: Advertising pixels, conversion tracking, remarketing tags

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our platform, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about each cookie category

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through:

  • iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security
  • Android Chrome: Chrome app → Settings → Site Settings → Cookies
  • Our Mobile Apps: In-app privacy settings

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • You may need to log in more frequently
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some features may not work properly
  • Personalized content may not be available

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
