COMPREHENSIVE FACULTY TOOLS

Everything Teachers Need to Succeed

Teachers, coaches, and club leaders can present their profiles, photos, calendars, links to valuable resources, lesson plans, and other important information in one logical place for students and parents to engage.

Parental Involvement

Teachers need to communicate more than just homework assignments to parents. We all know the more involved parent, the higher the achieving student. Therefore, putting an effective parent communication platform on top of Google Classroom makes sense.

Content Delivery

Through Google Classroom's feed, a teacher can deliver various content items to the class. However, many teachers need more than just a feed. Those teachers need ClassBlocks. In-line videos, document sharing and more are shared through both synchronous and asynchronous methodologies.

SSO + Security

Through the use of SIS, G Suite or Office 365 as our host of all account information, ClassBlocks intentionally avoids storing all usernames and passwords. It enables the highest possible level of security while removing a significant amount of complexity including the management of permissions and roles.
