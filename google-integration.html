Real-Time Google Integration for Ultimate Synchronization

SchoolBlocks goes 1,000 miles further than any other vendor to ensure that your school's data is always current and consistent across all platforms. We leverage Google's APIs and Webhooks in the following ways:

Calendar API Sync

Instantly synchronize your Google Calendars with the SchoolBlocks platform, ensuring all events, important dates, and schedule changes are immediately reflected. Never miss an update on school events, board meetings, or classroom schedules.

Drive API & Webhook Document Sync

Maintain a single source of truth for all school documents and folders. Our combined use of the Google Drive API and webhooks guarantees real-time synchronization, meaning any addition, update, or deletion in your Google Drive is mirrored in SchoolBlocks—and vice versa—automatically.

Display Google Calendar events directly on your school website. Show district-wide events, school-specific calendars, athletic schedules, and important dates. Your community stays informed with real-time calendar integration that automatically updates when changes are made in Google Calendar.

Embed Google Drive files and folders directly into your web pages. Share documents, presentations, forms, and resources without leaving your website. Students, parents, and staff can access important files with proper permissions, all managed through your existing Google Workspace structure.

Connect Google Classroom with your school website to showcase assignments, announcements, and class resources. Teachers can easily share classroom content with parents and the broader school community, creating transparency and engagement in the learning process.

Google Drive + Calendar content stays on your website.

Unlike basic embeds or external links, SchoolBlocks uses Google APIs to bring calendar events, files, and folders directly into your website pages. Your visitors never leave your site to access Google content - everything displays natively within your school's branded experience. This deep API integration keeps users engaged with your content instead of redirecting them away.
