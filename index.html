WEBSITES

SchoolBlocks transforms the wealth of information on school websites into a clear, engaging experience for families and staff alike. Designed with accessibility and simplicity in mind, it helps parents, students, and staff quickly find what they needwhile ensuring full ADA, WCAG 2.2, and CIPA compliance.

GOOGLE INTEGRATION

With seamless Google Workspace integration, SchoolBlocks brings your files, folders, and calendar events directly into each pagemaking your website both powerful and easy to manage. Combined with great pricing and responsive support, it's a winning formula for every K-12 district.

AI POWERED ANSWERS

Integrate our AI Answers companion to provide your members with instant, intelligent responses in natural languageexactly what they expect from modern technology. Enhance engagement, reduce support bottlenecks, and give your community a seamless way to get the information they need, when they need it.

SAFE SOCIAL PLATFORM

Turn social engagement into a positive experience. Our platform eliminates the common pitfalls of traditional social media while encouraging meaningful member-to-member interactions. With conversation prompts, leaderboards, and more, engaging with your organization becomes fun, rewarding, and safebuilding stronger connections and a thriving community.

DOCUMENT ENGAGEMENT

Make your policies, procedures, and handbooks clear, accessible, and easy to follow with DocMersion. Our newest innovation, DocMersion transforms core documents into fully searchable, translatable, and mobile-friendly pages, so everyone can quickly find and understand content such as policies. When people know what's expected, compliance improves and your organization fosters a safer, more harmonious environment for all.

MOBILE APPS

Launch your own app with our progressive web app (PWA) technology and see engagement soar. PWAs make your app instantly accessible, easy to use, and fastdriving up to 3 the usage compared to traditional apps. Deliver a seamless mobile experience that keeps your community coming back.
